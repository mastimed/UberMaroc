Uber vs InDrive: Which App to Use in Morocco (2025)?

By UberMaroc Team • Updated: Nov 28, 2024
In this article:
  • 1. Price: Fixed or Negotiated?
  • 2. Car Quality & Comfort
  • 3. Availability (Casa, Marrakech)
  • 4. Final Verdict

Arriving at Mohammed V Airport or Marrakech Train Station can be stressful. Between taxi drivers refusing to use the meter and the language barrier, ride-hailing apps are essential. But between Uber (often operating as Careem or Heetch depending on the season) and InDrive, who wins?

1. Price: Fixed vs Negotiation

This is the major difference between the two giants.

  • Uber/Careem: The price is fixed. You know exactly what you will pay before you get in. Ideal if you don't know local rates.
  • InDrive: Based on an auction system. You propose a price, drivers accept or counter-offer.

2. Car Quality and Safety

Uber/Careem generally enforces stricter standards for vehicles (usually under 5 years old). AC is often mandatory.

On InDrive, standards are looser. You might get a brand new car or an old Peugeot. Always check the driver's rating before accepting.

4. Verdict: Which one to choose?

If you are a tourist wanting peace of mind, choose Uber (via our promo link). It's safer and support is better.
